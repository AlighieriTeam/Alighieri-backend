What is Alighieri?

Welcome to Alighieri - a platform for testing artificial intelligence algorithms on multiplayer games. With this platform, which allows you to run algorithms locally on your computer and then send the necessary commands using our API, you can test your algorithms and compete with your friends. Join our community today and start developing your projects!

About us

We are a young dynamic student team with interests focused broadly on artificial intelligence. We are aware of the fact that when writing algorithms or bots based on AI, there will often appear all sorts of problems that are hard to see at first glance and which cause discouragement in this exciting topic. For this reason, we have decided together to build a platform for testing artificial intelligence algorithms written by users.
